Fascinating Crocodile Facts

Diet: Crocodiles are carnivores, feasting on fish, birds, mammals, and even large animals like wildebeests that wander too close to the water.

Habitat: Found in tropical swamps, rivers, lakes, and coastal regions, some crocodile species can tolerate saltwater, making them versatile survivors.

Behavior: As cold-blooded reptiles, they bask in the sun for warmth and submerge in water to cool down, perfectly adapting to their environment.

Interesting Fact: Crocodiles have been around since the age of dinosaurs and can live over 100 years. Their jaw strength is one of the most powerful on Earth.
